About Galoria

Galoria was founded by Jefferson Wiseman. A student at a community college

looking to further his skills in website development while also helping others

their projects. He had an idea to make a website that would allow artistic students

to showcase digital art for other people to see. After a successful presentation

his stakeholders, Jefferson wanted to expand his website.He wanted to make a responsive

website for digital artists around to showcase their creations. Thats when he made Galoria.

Please feel free to share our social media links below so that more viewers and

artists can know about us.
